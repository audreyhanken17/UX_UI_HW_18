About AudreyHi, I’m Audrey! I’m an avid doodler ✍️  a street photographer 📷  a cat lover 🐈 and a problem-solver ✅. I’m all about creating a lasting and meaningful impact on people’s experiences through good design and I’m fueled by a deep curiosity and desire to solve complex problems in impactful ways. I’ve worn many hats throughout my career – administrative, marketing, sales, leadership, instructional design, graphic design – you name it. Out of all of them, I’ve found that the creative hat suits me best and realized that I can contribute something completely different to the design industry. Something that stems from storytelling, imagination, and innovation. Outside of work, I seek inspiration from nature, architecture, and technology, and love to experiment with new mediums and techniques. I’m passionate about diversity, equality, and empathy, and strive to incorporate these values into my way of work.
